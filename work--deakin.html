Work completed for HardHat Digital whilst freelancing in Melbourne 2014-2015. Deakin University ATAR Calculator was produced in the studio. I was the lead UI/UX Designer.

Client Needs

Meetings with the client at HardHat Digital were to show exactly work to be completed in low fidelity and high fidelity wireframes. Once the wireframes were signed off by the client the website development began.

Design Value

The project was marked out with mobile-first responsive design and a key which showed the results from the ATAR scores.

Design value was added by showing colour coding for the key of results. Therefore directing students to the approach course.
